The Helping Hand Project

The Helping Hand Project is a Melbourne based mentoring program that helps young refugees succeed in life and to follow their dreams. The successful program is an initiative from the
Farnham Street Neighbourhood Learning Centre.

The Helping Hand Project is run by a small, dedicated team of volunteers who believe passionately in the benefits that mentoring can offer the wider community.

Although based in the Flemington/Kensington area, the program also meets the needs of surrounding communities.
